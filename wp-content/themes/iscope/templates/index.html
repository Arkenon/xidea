ABOUT US

iScope Concussion and

Pain Clinics

iScope Concussion and Pain Clinics is Canada’s only network of interdisciplinary centres providing concussion rehabilitation and pain management. Our specialist-led team is comprised of sub-specialized physicians, nurse practitioners, rehabilitation professionals and technologists that work collaboratively to help treat the physical, cognitive, emotional, and sleep-related symptoms of concussion, pain and neurological injuries.

What We Treat

The following are some of the common neurological conditions that we treat. Please note that this list is not exhaustive. We welcome all referrals and will work alongside of our patients to ensure they are seen by the appropriate practitioner.

  • Concussion (mTBI)
  • Headache & Migraine
  • Chronic Pain
  • Vertigo & Dizziness
  • Parkinson's Disease & Movement Disorders
  • Seizures & Epilepsy
  • Memory Impairments & Dementia
  • Visual Dysfunction
  • & More

New Patients


iScope is accepting new patients. A referral from your primary care physician or specialist is required for consultations covered by your provincial healthcare plan. If you require rehabilitation services, such as physiotherapy or kinesiology, a referral is not required.
